About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta with Prof. Sourav Bhattacharya. My research interests are Political Economy, Microeconomic Theory, and Game Theory. My current research explores the political economy of identity, with a focus on how identity and group-specific issues impact political competition and campaigns.

I completed my M.Sc. in Economics from IIT Roorkee, where I did my master's thesis with Prof. Rachita Gulati. I completed my B.Sc. in Mathematics from Savitribai Phule Pune University.
